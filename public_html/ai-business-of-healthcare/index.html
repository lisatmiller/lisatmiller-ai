Revenue Cycle and Billing

Denial prevention, not denial recovery

$20B spent yearly pursuing denial appeals. AI can scrub claims before submission, verify eligibility in real time, and prevent denials before they happen.

  • Automated eligibility verification
  • Claim scrubbing before submission
  • Denial pattern detection and prevention
  • Appeals automation

Clinical Documentation and Coding

From dictation to structured data

Physicians spend hours on documentation that could go to patients. AI can capture, structure, and code clinical encounters in real time, reducing burnout and improving accuracy.

  • AI-assisted clinical documentation
  • Automated medical coding
  • CDI query generation
  • Chart review automation
03

Patient Access and Scheduling

From intake to appointment

Patients wait because intake, scheduling, and insurance verification are manual. AI can handle the handoffs so patients move through faster and staff focus on care.

  • Automated intake and registration
  • Smart scheduling and capacity matching
  • Insurance verification before the visit
  • Automated reminders and no-show prevention

Supply Chain and Procurement

From ordering to optimization

Supply costs are a hospital's second-largest expense after labor. AI can predict demand, automate reordering, and flag spend anomalies before they become budget problems.

  • Demand forecasting and automated reordering
  • Inventory level monitoring
  • Vendor price comparison and contract compliance
  • Spend anomaly detection

Quality, Safety, and Compliance

From audits to real-time monitoring

Quality reporting is retrospective and manual. AI can monitor in real time, flag risks before they become events, and automate the reporting that consumes staff hours.

  • Real-time quality metric monitoring
  • Automated regulatory reporting
  • Risk pattern detection and alerting
  • Compliance audit preparation

Workforce and HR Operations

Capacity returned to your people

Scheduling, credentialing, onboarding, and staff communication run on manual processes. AI absorbs the routine so your HR team focuses on people, not paperwork.

  • Credentialing and compliance tracking
  • Onboarding workflow automation

Capacity Forecasting and Utilization

Right staff, right room, right time

Census forecasting and room utilization run on spreadsheets and instinct. AI can predict demand, match staffing to census, and surface underutilized capacity before it becomes lost revenue.

What health system leaders ask

What is the business of healthcare AI? +

The business of healthcare AI focuses on operational outcomes: automating manual workflows like intake and insurance verification, connecting disconnected systems, and responding to leads in seconds.

How does AI automate healthcare workflows? +

AI connects your EHR, CRM, billing system, and phone system. It handles insurance verification, patient intake, document routing, and report generation without manual data entry.

What is speed to lead in healthcare? +

Speed to lead means responding to new patient inquiries in seconds. The practice that responds first typically wins the appointment. AI makes instant follow-up automatic.

Can AI integrate with our EHR? +

Yes. Most modern EHRs support API integration. We connect your EHR with your phone system, CRM, and other tools so data flows automatically.

Is workflow automation HIPAA-aware? +

Yes. All integrations and automations are designed with HIPAA awareness. Data handling follows healthcare privacy requirements.

How long does installation take? +

Most systems are installed and running in about 30 days, depending on the number of integrations and workflow complexity.
