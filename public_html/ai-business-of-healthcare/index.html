01

Revenue Cycle

Denial prevention, not denial recovery

$20B spent yearly pursuing denial appeals. Clinical staff time consumed chasing claims that should have been resolved before submission. AI can prevent denials before they happen.

  • Automated eligibility verification
  • Claim scrubbing before submission
  • Denial pattern detection and prevention
  • Appeals automation for unavoidable denials

Prior Authorization

13 hours a week, per physician

Physicians and staff spend 13 hours weekly on prior auth. Hours that should go to patients, consumed by paperwork and phone calls. AI can absorb most of that work.

  • Automated auth submission and tracking
  • Real-time status checking
  • Document collection and routing
  • Alert on expiring authorizations
03

Patient Access

From intake to scheduling

Patients wait because intake, scheduling, and insurance verification are manual processes. AI can handle the handoffs so patients move through faster.

  • Automated intake and registration
  • Insurance verification before the visit
  • Smart scheduling and reminders
  • Reduced no-show impact

Supply Chain

What health system leaders ask

What is the business of healthcare AI? +

The business of healthcare AI focuses on operational outcomes: automating manual workflows like intake and insurance verification, connecting disconnected systems, and responding to leads in seconds.

How does AI automate healthcare workflows? +

AI connects your EHR, CRM, billing system, and phone system. It handles insurance verification, patient intake, document routing, and report generation without manual data entry.

What is speed to lead in healthcare? +

Speed to lead means responding to new patient inquiries in seconds. The practice that responds first typically wins the appointment. AI makes instant follow-up automatic.

Can AI integrate with our EHR? +

Yes. Most modern EHRs support API integration. We connect your EHR with your phone system, CRM, and other tools so data flows automatically.

Is workflow automation HIPAA-aware? +

Yes. All integrations and automations are designed with HIPAA awareness. Data handling follows healthcare privacy requirements.

How long does installation take? +

Most systems are installed and running in about 30 days, depending on the number of integrations and workflow complexity.
