How is AI being used in healthcare? +

AI is used in healthcare for automated patient call answering, appointment scheduling and reminders, dormant patient recall campaigns, insurance verification, clinical documentation, and patient acquisition through AI search visibility. The most impactful applications fix revenue leaks first.

What is the business of healthcare AI? +

The business of healthcare AI focuses on operational outcomes: reducing no-shows, recovering missed call revenue, automating manual workflows like intake and insurance verification, and making practices findable when patients ask AI engines for recommendations.

Is the AI work HIPAA-aware? +

Yes. All systems Lisa installs are designed with HIPAA awareness. Patient-facing automation uses compliant tools and processes, and data handling follows healthcare privacy requirements.

Most systems are installed, tested, and running in about 30 days. The timeline depends on the number of systems being deployed and the complexity of your existing workflows.
