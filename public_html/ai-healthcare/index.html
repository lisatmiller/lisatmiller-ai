Questions about AI in healthcare

How is AI used in healthcare practices? +

AI is used for 24/7 patient call answering, automated appointment scheduling and reminders, dormant patient recall campaigns, and AI search visibility so patients find your practice when they ask AI engines for recommendations.

What AI tools answer patient calls? +

AI voice agents like Rosie, Goodcall, and Ringly answer patient calls 24/7, schedule appointments, route emergencies, and log every interaction. They integrate with practice management systems.

Can AI recall dormant patients? +

Yes. Automated recall campaigns use email, SMS, and voice to reach patients overdue for visits. Campaigns are segmented by visit type and interval, and results are tracked automatically.

Is AI call answering HIPAA-aware? +

Yes. The tools we use are designed with HIPAA awareness. Patient data is handled through compliant processes, and the systems can be configured to meet your practice's privacy requirements.

What is AEO for healthcare? +

AEO (Answer Engine Optimization) makes your practice visible when patients ask ChatGPT or Google AI for a healthcare recommendation. It involves structured data, AI-optimized content, and AI crawler access.

Costs vary based on scope. The first step is a free strategy call to diagnose your revenue leaks and determine which systems deliver the fastest return.
