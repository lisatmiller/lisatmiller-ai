I started my career in healthcare sales and never left. Over 25 years, I worked inside hospitals, health systems, and the companies that serve them. I learned how decisions get made, who makes them, and what happens when the workflow breaks down.

At VIE Healthcare, I generated $250 million in sales without outside investment. I didn't do that by selling harder. I did it by understanding the numbers, fixing the breakdowns, and building systems that produced consistent results.

The shift to AI

When AI became practical for small healthcare practices, I saw something most people missed. The practices that needed AI most weren't the ones adopting it. They were too busy answering calls, chasing no-shows, and retyping intake forms.

So I started doing what I've always done. Diagnosing the revenue leaks. Installing the systems that fix them. Measuring the results. The tools changed. The approach didn't.

What I do today

I help healthcare practices and organizations use AI to fix revenue leaks, automate workflows, and become visible in AI search. Through TechStack Strategy, I install the systems. Through the Selling to Healthcare podcast, I share what I learn.

The practices that win with AI aren't the ones with the most technology. They're the ones who fix the right breakdowns first.Lisa T. Miller

The book

I authored The Executive YES, a book about healthcare sales and leadership. The principles in it, knowing your numbers, understanding your customer, building systems that scale, are the same ones I apply to AI in healthcare today.
